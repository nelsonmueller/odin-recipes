The Nutella Bread

The first Nutella bread was invented in 1964 and has since been a breakfast of winners and losers alike. The taste of the Nutella chocolate spread combined with a fresh bread slice is one of the best inventions of the 20 century. To create this special dish it needs usually a connoisseur and a thorough understanding of chocolate and bread. Only then, one will be able to create a perfect nutella bread. But here, we will tell you how you can achieve it on your own.

Ingredients:

  • 1 Nutella jar
  • 1 slice of white bread

Steps to the perfect Nutella bread:

  1. Let it rest: The bread has to get used to the surroundings of the plate.
  2. Let it breath: Open the Nutella jar and let it breath shortly.
  3. Spread the Nutella: Using a knife, take a good amount of Nutella from the jar and spread it all over the sliced bread. Repeat this 2-3 times until you can't see the surface of the bread anymore.
